Puran Poli Recipe

  • Chana dal (split Bengal gram)
  • Jaggery
  • Cardamom & nutmeg powder
  • Wheat flour or maida
  • Turmeric, salt, oil or ghee

üßë‚Äçüç≥ Procedure

  • Boil chana dal, drain excess water, and mash it well.
  • Cook mashed dal with jaggery until it thickens into a filling (called puran). Add cardamom and nutmeg.
  • Knead soft dough using wheat flour, turmeric, salt, and water. Rest it for 30 mins.
  • Roll a small dough ball, fill with puran, and gently roll into a flat poli.
  • Roast on a tawa with ghee until golden spots appear.
  • Serve hot with ghee or milk!
